Historical bakground of school

Holy family girls' secondary school started to operate since December 2011. Our school is located in Mwanza at Ilemela distrit in Nyegezi.
As for students from other regions they take a bus and received within the campus.

Levels of education and curriculum

Our school provides ordinary level of education from form one to form four also pre form one courses.We admit all students as they are able to adhere by the rule since its a catholic school, we use learner centered curriculum where we base on the understanding of the student

Number of stuff and subjects taught

We have about 32 staff members both teaching and non-teaching stuff also the number of subjects taught is 10 including both science, art and religious subjects

The vision and mission

Our schools' vision and mission is to ensure love, responsibility and unity among our students for their better future.
